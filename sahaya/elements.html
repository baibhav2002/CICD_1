Handicrafts

Visit and discover some of the exceptional crafts by our local artisans.

Some places nearby city:

  1. 1.Sudharshan Nagar Arts Village

    This place is famous for its carved wood, stone and fibre-glass sculpture works

  2. 2.Utkalika (Govt. Of Odisha Handicrafts)

    This place is famous for Tribal Jewellery, Applique, Patta & Tassar Paintings, Stone Carving, Wood Carving, Palm Leaf Painting, Jute Craft, Golden Grass Craft, Sabai Grass Craft

  3. 3.Orient crafts museum

    This place is famous for stone, wood crafts and handicrafts

  4. 4.Odisha Crafts Museum(Kalabhoomi)

    This place is famous for galleries for terracotta, traditional painting, stone and wood crafts, metal crafts, and handloom. Courtyards dedicated to tribal living and temple architecture are part of the museum complex.

IMPORTANT

Accepted:
Promoting these local run crafts centers are accepted. We encourage people to buy these products to promote better living conditions for these people. Wholesalers are also encouraged to buy in bulk and connect these people from the rural to the urban centres.
Not Accepted:
We won’t accept any kind of unethical means of practices like cheating the people by not paying suitable prices of their hardwork. Fake orders in bulk. No middlemen allowed.
